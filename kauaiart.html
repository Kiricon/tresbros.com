The Art Work  of Eduardo Valenciana
PO BOX 3655     LIHUE     HAWAII     96766           808-652-6161
Edaurdo began his painting career at the age of four in the barrio of East Los Angeles.  While displaying his award winning canvas' at age ten, his parents and the boy were invited to study under the guiding eye of renowned artist James Bowman.  Trained initially as a portrait artist and working in oils, Eduardo has utilized many different mediums and tools in reflecting his passion and emotions over the years.  A resident of the island of Kauai for the past 26 years, he has been deeply influenced by the radiance of the islands.  Please feel free to contact him with any questions you may have.
On October 31, 1979, Eduardo was the surviving Flight Attendant on board Western Airlines DC-10 Flight 2605 which crashed on landing at Mexico City.  The incident influenced his artwork greatly and is currently the only artist combining aviation and marine life in his works.  Please click the appropriate gallery.
