BLACK BOX: A TRUE TALE OF TWISTED FATE
On October 31, 1979, Eduardo was a Flight Attendant on Western Airlines DC-10 flight 2605 to Mexico City.  The jumbo jet crashed on landing.  He  was one of but a few survivors.  He utilized his artistic talents to express the emotional and mental roller-coasted ride he would have to endure for the next ten years.  The following are a glimpse of some of the canvas works he created during this difficult time.
